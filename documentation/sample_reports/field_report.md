## Conclusions

The field's net pay estimates suggest a moderate to high degree of uncertainty, with P10 and P50 values bracketing the range between 307 and 416 meters. The upper bound of this range, at 653.9 meters, is significantly higher than the other two estimates, indicating that there may be some outliers or anomalous data points contributing to this value. Given these results, it appears that the field's net pay is likely in the order of hundreds of meters, but further investigation and more detailed analysis are needed to better constrain this estimate. Next steps should focus on acquiring additional well control and refining the reservoir model to reduce uncertainty and improve the accuracy of future predictions.
